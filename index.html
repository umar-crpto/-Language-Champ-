AI Language Assistant Add a chatbot tutor where users can: - ask grammar questions - practice conversation - translate sentences - get explanations 10. AI Homework Helper Students can upload a question and get explanations. 2. Smart Study Planner AI creates a daily language learning schedule. 3.
